OROBOUND

Orobound is my hardware hackathon project. The objective of this project is to detect pneumonia through breathing into a non-invasive device. Throughout the weekend, I have done tons of research on various biosensors to detect pneumonia virus. Unfortunately, given the time duration of the hackathon, we were unable to integrate the biosensors into our device, and subsituted the biosensors with humidity sensors. We used various hardware platforms such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi to create a prototype sensing humidity levels in the surrounding area
